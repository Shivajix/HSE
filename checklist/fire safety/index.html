Essential Fire Safety Tips

Smoke Detectors

Test smoke detectors monthly and replace batteries annually. Install detectors on every level and in every sleeping area.

Fire Extinguishers

Place ABC-type extinguishers in key locations. Train staff on PASS technique (Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep).

Clear Exits

Keep emergency exits unobstructed and clearly marked. Ensure exit signs are illuminated and visible.

Electrical Safety

Avoid overloading circuits. Check for damaged cords and replace them immediately. Use surge protectors.

Evacuation Plans

Develop and practice evacuation drills regularly. Establish meeting points and assign evacuation helpers.

Combustible Materials

Store flammable liquids properly. Keep work areas clean and free of excessive combustible materials.
