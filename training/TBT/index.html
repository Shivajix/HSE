Effective TBT Meeting Structure

Introduction

Start with a clear topic introduction and explain why it's relevant to today's work activities.

Discussion Points

Cover key safety points, procedures, and potential hazards related to the topic.

Worker Participation

Encourage questions, experiences, and suggestions from all meeting participants.

Action Items

Identify specific safety actions or behaviors to implement immediately after the meeting.

Documentation

Record attendance, discussion points, and action items for compliance and follow-up.

Follow-up

Review previous TBT topics and check on implementation of previous action items.
