Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Privacy Commitment

At HSE Tools, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and tools.

Key Privacy Feature

All your data stays in your browser - we don't store any personal information on our servers.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information:

  • Email address (only when you contact us)
  • Name (only when you contact us)
  • Technical data (browser type, IP address)

Tool Data

Data you enter in our HSE tools:

  • Stays locally in your browser
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Deleted when you clear browser data

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our HSE tools
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Ensure website security and prevent fraud

Analytics

We use anonymous analytics to:

  • Understand how tools are used
  • Improve user experience
  • Identify popular features

Data Protection

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear browser data to remove tool inputs
  • Contact us to delete personal information
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Third-Party Services
