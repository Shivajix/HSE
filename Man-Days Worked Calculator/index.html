Man-Days Calculation Methods

Basic Man-Days Calculation

Man-Days = Number of Workers × Number of Days Worked

The simplest method for calculating total man-days, suitable for projects with consistent workforce and standard workdays.

Hours-Based Calculation

Man-Days = Total Hours Worked ÷ Standard Hours per Day

More accurate method that accounts for variations in daily work hours and overtime, converting total hours to equivalent man-days.

Productivity-Adjusted Calculation

Effective Man-Days = Actual Man-Days × Productivity Factor

Advanced method that adjusts for workforce productivity variations, providing more realistic project planning estimates.
