Effective Safety Induction Process

Pre-Employment Assessment

Evaluate new employee's previous safety training and identify specific induction needs based on role and experience.

Theoretical Training

Deliver comprehensive safety information through presentations, videos, and interactive learning modules.

Site Orientation

Conduct physical tour of workplace highlighting specific hazards, safety equipment, and emergency facilities.

Practical Demonstration

Show correct use of safety equipment, emergency procedures, and safe work practices through hands-on demonstration.

Assessment & Sign-off

Evaluate understanding through written or practical assessment and obtain signed acknowledgment of training completion.

Follow-up & Refresher

Schedule follow-up sessions and periodic refresher training to reinforce safety knowledge and address new hazards.
