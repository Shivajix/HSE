Essential Office Safety Tips

Ergonomic Workstations

Ensure chairs, desks, and monitors are properly adjusted to prevent strain injuries and promote good posture.

Electrical Safety

Regularly inspect cords and outlets, avoid overloading circuits, and use surge protectors where needed.

Slip & Trip Prevention

Keep walkways clear, secure loose cables, and clean spills immediately to prevent falls and injuries.

Fire Safety

Maintain clear emergency exits, ensure fire extinguishers are accessible, and conduct regular fire drills.

Eye Strain Prevention

Implement the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds) to reduce digital eye strain.

Health & Hygiene

Promote regular hand washing, provide sanitizing stations, and encourage sick employees to work from home.
