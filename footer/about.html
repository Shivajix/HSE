Our Story

HSE Tools was born from a simple observation: safety professionals spend too much time on paperwork and calculations, taking away from their core mission - keeping people safe. I noticed this pattern across multiple industries and decided to create a solution.

Starting with basic calculators, the platform has grown to include comprehensive risk assessment tools, reporting systems, and safety documentation generators. Every tool is designed with practicality in mind - no fluff, just what you need to get the job done efficiently.

Our Mission

To democratize access to professional HSE tools by providing free, practical, and easy-to-use solutions that enhance workplace safety and streamline safety management processes.

Our Vision

A world where every safety professional, regardless of budget or resources, has access to the tools they need to create safer work environments and protect workers effectively.
