JSA Process Steps

1

Select the Job

Choose a job to analyze, prioritizing those with the highest injury rates, potential for severe injury, or history of incidents.

2

Break Down the Job

Divide the job into basic steps in sequence of operation. Each step should describe what is done, not how it is done.

3

Identify Hazards

For each step, identify potential hazards including chemical, physical, biological, ergonomic, and psychological hazards.

4

Develop Controls

Determine preventive measures to eliminate or reduce identified hazards, following the hierarchy of controls.

5

Implement & Review

Put controls in place, train workers, and regularly review the JSA to ensure its effectiveness and update as needed.
